# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Tech-savvy startup founders and enterprise CTOs/product leaders seeking custom software, AI automation, cloud solutions, and digital transformation services. They operate in high-stakes, fast-moving environments where every day without the right partner costs them market share. They are pragmatic decision-makers who value competence demonstrated through case studies, technical depth, and clear communication of complexity without jargon. They're evaluating multiple vendors; speed, credibility, and intellectual confidence matter more than flashiness.

## Product Purpose

Eleviq Technologies is an engineering and consulting firm that transforms organizations through digital intelligence—custom software, AI/automation, cloud architecture, web/mobile products, and strategic IT advisory. The website and brand system must communicate technical excellence, innovation partnership, and the ability to handle complex transformations. Success = prospects booking discovery calls, existing clients returning for additional work, and tier-1 talent applying to join.

## Brand Personality

**Three words:** Capable, Forward-thinking, Grounded

*Capable*: Deep expertise, shipped work, confidence without arrogance. Eleviq solves hard problems; the visual system should reflect that quiet mastery.

*Forward-thinking*: AI/automation first, not an afterthought. The design leans into possibility and innovation, not nostalgia or trend-chasing.

*Grounded*: African identity (Nairobi-based) and pragmatism. No Silicon Valley clichés. The design respects the client's reality: outcomes matter, time is money, implementation is everything.

## Anti-references

**DO NOT:**
- Resemble generic SaaS landing pages (Stripe-minimal, Notion-ish, editorial-magazine serif eyebrow scaffolding)
- Use cream/beige/warm-neutral body backgrounds (saturated AI default; signals mediocrity by 2026)
- Default to Fraunces, Newsreader, or DM Sans (training-data reflexes)
- Rely on centered hero sections with big metrics + gradient accent (SaaS cliché)
- Use dark mode as the default (not justified by this context; tech services don't require dark-mode-as-aesthetic)
- Over-decorate with glassmorphism, blur, or decorative gradients (distracts from competence signal)
- Use tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section (2023 template grammar)

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** Demonstrate capability through project imagery, case studies, client results, and technical depth. "We're expert in AI" is noise; a detailed architecture diagram + client outcome is proof.

2. **Respect the viewer's time.** Executives are busy. Clear CTA hierarchy, fast navigation, and immediate value proposition win over clever micro-interactions or scroll-bait.

3. **Typography carries expertise.** Font choice and hierarchy are the primary signal of craftsmanship. One well-chosen font family with committed weight/size contrast beats generic pairing.

4. **Color IS voice; use it deliberately.** Cyan (provided) signals innovation and future-readiness. Navy establishes authority and trust. The palette should commit to these, not dilute them with beige.

5. **Proximity to the user's needs.** Every design choice should reflect the fact that visitors are evaluating us, and evaluation speed matters. Fast-loading, responsive, accessible experiences aren't nice-to-haves; they're baseline.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.1 AA standard** (minimum). All interactive elements, forms, and key CTAs must meet ≥4.5:1 contrast.
- **Reduced motion:** Every animation must have a `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` fallback (crossfade, no motion, or instant transition).
- **Color-blind friendly:** Cyan and Navy are distinguishable in all common color-blindness types (protanopia, deuteranopia, tritanopia). Never rely on color alone to convey information.
- **Keyboard-navigable:** All CTAs, forms, and navigation must be reachable via Tab, Enter, Esc. Focus states visible at ≥3:1 contrast.
- **Responsive first:** Tested down to 320px width (mobile-first breakpoints).
- **Loading performance:** LCP ≤ 2.5s, CLS ≤ 0.1, FID ≤ 100ms. Core Web Vitals green.
